Features

Built for the way
doctors actually work.

Ambient Recording

No buttons to press, no scripts to follow. Just consult naturally and let the AI work invisibly in the background.

Real-Time Transcription

Groq-accelerated Whisper processes audio with industry-leading latency. Hindi, English, and code-switching supported natively.

SOAP Note Generation

Automatically structured Subjective, Objective, Assessment, and Plan notes — reviewed and editable before saving.

ICD-10 / SNOMED Coding

Clinical entities are automatically mapped to international standard codes — zero manual billing errors, zero extra steps.

FHIR R4 Bundles

Every approved encounter generates a fully compliant FHIR R4 bundle ready for interoperability and ABDM push.

Physician-in-Loop

Zero data is ever saved without your explicit approval. Ethical AI by design — the doctor is always the final authority.
